# Shopify Sales & Customer Funnel Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Shopify e-commerce sales data to understand **sales performance, customer behavior, retention, and repeat purchase trends**.  
The dashboard provides a complete view of the **customer funnel**, helping businesses make data-driven decisions to improve **revenue and customer loyalty**.

The project demonstrates **practical data analytics, KPI tracking, and business insight generation** using Power BI
## Business Objectives
- Evaluate overall sales performance  
- Analyze customer funnel and retention  
- Identify repeat customer behavior  
- Measure Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)  
- Understand payment gateway preferences  
- Analyze regional and city-level performance  
## Dataset Description
The dataset consists of Shopify transactional records including:
- Customer and order details  
- Product types and quantities  
- Payment gateways  
- Net sales and total price  
- Billing country, province, and city  
- Customer purchase frequency  

This dataset supports **sales analysis, customer segmentation, and retention analytics**.

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Excel / CSV (Shopify export data)  
## Key KPIs
- **Net Sales:** $4.18M  
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 7,534  
- **Net Average Order Value:** $562.6  
- **Total Customers:** 4,431  
- **Repeat Customers:** 2,039  
- **Repeat Rate:** 46.02%  
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):** $943.6  
- **Purchase Frequency:** 2  
## Dashboard Insights
### Sales Performance
- Strong overall revenue with a high average order value  
- Indicates customers are purchasing high-value products  
### Customer Funnel Analysis
- Clear distinction between single-order and repeat customers  
- Nearly half of customers return, showing strong retention  
### Repeat Customer Trend
- Repeat purchases show a consistent upward trend  
- Reflects increasing customer loyalty over time
### Payment Gateway Analysis
- Shopify Payments is the most preferred payment method  
- Followed by PayPal and Gift Cards  
- Helps optimize checkout and payment strategy
### Product Type Analysis
Top products driving repeat purchases:
- Running Shoes  
- Tennis Shoes  
- Walking Shoes  
- Climbing Shoes  

These products contribute significantly to customer retention.

### Regional & City Analysis
- High repeat-customer concentration in major U.S. cities  
- Useful for targeted marketing and regional planning

 ## Business Recommendations
- Focus on improving repeat customer retention  
- Promote high-performing product categories  
- Strengthen loyalty programs to increase purchase frequency  
- Optimize Shopify Payments due to high customer usage  
- Target high-retention regions for marketing campaigns

##  Overview

The **Drillthrough Dashboard** provides a **detailed, transaction-level view** of Shopify sales data.
While the main dashboard highlights **high-level KPIs, trends, and customer insights**, this drillthrough page enables users to **deep-dive into individual customer orders** for detailed investigation, validation, and analysis.
This feature strengthens the overall analytics solution by supporting **root-cause analysis** and **granular data exploration**.

##  Purpose of Drillthrough
The drillthrough dashboard is designed to:
- Analyze **individual customer transactions**
- Validate **aggregated KPIs** at the order level
- Investigate specific **customers, products, cities, or regions**
- Support **data auditing, validation, and business verification**

##  Fields Available in Drillthrough View
The drillthrough table provides complete transaction transparency with the following attributes:
- Billing Address Country  
- Customer Name  
- Customer ID  
- Order Number  
- City  
- Billing Address ZIP Code  
- Product Type  
- Payment Gateway  
- Net Sales  
- Total Price (USD)  
- Total Tax  

This structure ensures a **clear link between summary metrics and raw transactional data**

##  Key Use Cases

- Identify **high-value customers** and analyze their purchase behavior  
- Evaluate **product-level performance**, especially for repeat customers  
- Verify **payment gateway usage** at the transaction level  
- Investigate **city-wise or region-wise sales anomalies**  
- Perform **customer-level revenue tracking and validation**

##  Business Value

- Bridges the gap between **summary dashboards and raw data**
- Enables **data-driven validation of KPIs**
- Improves **trust and transparency** in reported insights
- Supports **operational, sales, and customer-level analysis**
- Helps stakeholders confidently drill from **strategic insights to detailed records**
